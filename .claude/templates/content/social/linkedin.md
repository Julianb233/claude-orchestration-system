## Hook Formulas

**Question Hook:**
- "What if I told you [unexpected truth]?"
- "Why do [successful people] always [action]?"

**Statement Hook:**
- "Stop doing [common mistake]."
- "This [simple thing] changed my [result]."

**Curiosity Hook:**
- "Nobody talks about this, but..."
- "The real reason [thing happens]..."

**Stat Hook:**
- "[X]% of [people] don't know this."
- "After analyzing [number] [things], I found..."

## Best Practices

- Use line breaks for readability
- First-person perspective
- Share insights, not just announcements
- Ask questions to drive comments
- 1,300-1,500 characters optimal

## Character Limits

| Element | Limit |
|---------|-------|
| Total characters | 3,000 |
| Visible before fold | ~700 |
| Optimal length | 1,300-1,500 |
| Hashtags | 3-5 max |

## Post Checklist

- [ ] Hook is compelling in first 2 lines
- [ ] Line breaks every 2-3 lines
- [ ] Tells a story or shares insight
- [ ] First-person perspective
- [ ] Clear takeaway or lesson
- [ ] Ends with CTA or question
- [ ] 3-5 relevant hashtags
- [ ] No external links (kills reach)
