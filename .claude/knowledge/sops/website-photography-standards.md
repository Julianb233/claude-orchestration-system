# Website Photography Standards SOP

**Owner:** Julian
**Created:** 2024-12-24
**Applies to:** All client website projects

---

## Image Quality Standards

### Camera & Lens Requirements
All website images must appear as if captured with:
- **Camera:** Sony A7IV (or equivalent full-frame professional camera)
- **Lens:** G Master series or equivalent professional glass
- **Aperture:** f/1.2 to f/1.4 maximum (nothing less - creates beautiful bokeh and subject separation)

### Style Guidelines
- **Candid photography style** - natural, unposed moments
- **Professional quality** - sharp focus on subject, creamy bokeh background
- **Emotional connection** - images that tell a story and connect with viewers

### Purpose-Driven Selection
Every image must:
1. **Be relevant** to the page content it appears on
2. **Support conversion** - help convince visitors to take action
3. **Match brand messaging** - align with company values and tone
4. **Feature real people** (or realistic stock that looks authentic)

---

## Image Requirements by Page Type

### Hero Sections
- Wide aspect ratio (16:9 or 21:9)
- High emotional impact
- Clear subject with shallow depth of field
- Person(s) should convey trust, warmth, professionalism

### Service Pages
- Show the service being delivered
- Candid moments between caregiver and client
- Warm lighting, natural colors
- Convey care, competence, compassion

### About/Team Pages
- Professional but approachable headshots
- Natural expressions (genuine smiles)
- Consistent lighting across team photos
- Environmental portraits showing context

### Location Pages
- Local landmarks or recognizable areas
- Community feel
- Mix of lifestyle and location imagery

### Testimonials/Reviews
- Real customer photos when available
- Authentic expressions
- Relatable situations

---

## Technical Specifications

### File Formats
- WebP preferred (with JPG fallback)
- AVIF for modern browsers where supported

### Resolution
- Hero images: 1920px minimum width, 2560px preferred
- Card images: 640px minimum, 1024px preferred
- Thumbnails: 320px minimum

### Optimization
- Lazy loading for below-fold images
- Blur placeholders for perceived performance
- srcset for responsive delivery

---

## AI Image Generation Guidelines

When generating images with AI (Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.):
- Prompt for "Sony A7IV" and "G Master lens" look
- Specify "f/1.2 aperture" or "f/1.4 bokeh"
- Request "candid" or "documentary style"
- Include "professional photography" and "natural lighting"
- Avoid stock photo clichés

### Example Prompts
```
Candid photo of a home health caregiver helping elderly woman,
shot on Sony A7IV with 50mm f/1.2 G Master lens, shallow depth
of field, natural window lighting, warm tones, documentary style,
professional healthcare photography --ar 16:9 --v 6
```

## Quality Checklist

Before adding an image to any website:
- [ ] Does it look like professional photography (not stock)?
- [ ] Is the subject in sharp focus with pleasing bokeh?
- [ ] Is it relevant to the page content?
- [ ] Does it support conversion goals?
- [ ] Is it optimized for web (format, size, lazy loading)?
- [ ] Does it have proper alt text for SEO/accessibility?

---

**Remember:** Every image is a conversion opportunity. Treat it as such.
