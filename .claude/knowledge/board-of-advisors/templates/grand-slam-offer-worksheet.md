## Step 1: Define the Dream Outcome

**What does your customer REALLY want?** (Not your product - the RESULT)

```
Dream Outcome: _______________________________________________

Make it specific:
- How much? _____________
- By when? _____________
- What transformation? _____________
```

**Dream Outcome Examples:**
- Not "weight loss course" → "Lose 30lbs in 90 days without giving up pizza"
- Not "business coaching" → "Add $100K to your revenue in the next quarter"
- Not "marketing services" → "Fill your calendar with 20 qualified leads per week"

## Step 7: Name Your Offer

**Naming Formula Options:**

1. **Result + Timeframe:**
   `"[Result] in [Time] [Method]"`
   Example: "6-Pack Abs in 6 Weeks System"

2. **Dream Client + Transformation:**
   `"The [Dream Client] [Transformation] Program"`
   Example: "The Busy Mom Fat Loss Formula"

3. **Unique Mechanism:**
   `"The [Unique Method] [Result]"`

## Final Checklist

Before launching, verify:

- [ ] Dream outcome is specific and measurable
- [ ] Addresses problems before, during, AND after
- [ ] Each problem has a solution
- [ ] Value stack totals 10x+ the price
- [ ] Guarantee removes all risk
- [ ] Scarcity/urgency is REAL (not manufactured)
- [ ] Name is compelling and descriptive
- [ ] Price feels like a "steal" given the value

---

## Quick Reference: Value Equation

```
                Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood
VALUE = ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                   Time Delay × Effort Required
```

**To maximize value, ask:**
- Can I make the dream outcome bigger/better?
- Can I increase proof/certainty it will work?
- Can I deliver results faster?
- Can I make it easier for them?
