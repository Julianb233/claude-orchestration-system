## Step 8: 5 Time Assassins Self-Check

**Which one are you?**

### The Staller
- [ ] Wait for "perfect" conditions
- [ ] Overthink decisions
- [ ] Analysis paralysis

**Fix:** Set "good enough" standards, time-box decisions

### The Speed Demon
- [ ] Rush through everything
- [ ] Create rework
- [ ] Miss details

**Fix:** Slow down, use checklists

### The Supervisor
- [ ] Micromanage everything
- [ ] Can't let go
- [ ] Review all work

**Fix:** Document processes, trust but verify weekly

### The Saver
- [ ] Hoard tasks to "save money"
- [ ] Do everything yourself
- [ ] Fear of spending on help

**Fix:** Calculate TRUE cost of your time

### The Self-Medicator
- [ ] Use busyness to avoid issues
- [ ] Escape into work
- [ ] Avoid real problems

**Fix:** Face the real issues, get support
